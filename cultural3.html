Analysis of Artifact

Hip-hop, aka Rap, has a very wide influence on the youths of today, teens primarily. This style of music began in the 1900s. The origin of it actually came from a much more mellow and relaxing style of music known that originated in Africa. This was known as Jazz. The evolution of hip-hop is truly amazing in actuality. Within such a seemingly short period of time, this genre transpired through a century and more. But one thing that still rang true about rap is the negativity that it can portray.
