Description of Issue

Teens can have a very weak state of mind and can be less then a little strong willed. That being said, one can only imagine how the not so optimistic messages carried within, actually the majority, of this style of music, can have its affect on kids within their teenage years. This affect is only strengthened by big time artist such as, Lil Wayne, Jay-Z, Wiz Khalifa, Kendrick Lamar, 50 Cent, Waka Flocka, 2 Chainz, Doughboyz Cashout and many other rap super stars. Such big names can influence kids even more so into listening and taking to heart the songs that the stars make. Thus being the media, who unbeknownst to many, influence the people and their day to actions consistently.
