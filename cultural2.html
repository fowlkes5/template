Introduction

Power derives in music. So powerful are these tunes that they can even alter the mindsets and moods of those who listen to them. Some may be peaceful, joyful, and positive, while others, may have very negative connotations that transfer through the notes or words. Hip-hop, otherwise known nowadays as Rap, and Rock, are the two primary genres that are most likely to have negativity engraved within their lyrics. Which in turn, can affect those who listen to them.
